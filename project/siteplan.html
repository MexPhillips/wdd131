WDD 131 Project Site Plan

MEX GAMING ARENA

1. Site Name & Branding

Site Name: MEX GAMING ARENA

Description: The name is chosen to convey a sense of excitement ("Arena") and competition in the gaming space, using a short, memorable prefix ("MEX"). It clearly identifies the site as a central hub for competitive online gaming. The logo will be a stylized geometric design with neon accents.

Optional Domain: mexgamingarena.com

2. Site Purpose

The primary purpose of the MEX GAMING ARENA website is to serve as the **central, high-energy hub for competitive online gaming and esports organization.**

It provides a centralized platform for users to:

  • View, register for, and track the progress of various tournaments.
  • Browse and sort a comprehensive player directory and competitive leaderboard.
  • Find live match streams and connect with the organization for support or partnerships.

3. Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

The target audience consists of competitive gamers (16-30) interested in playing or watching structured esports tournaments.

  • "What competitive tournaments are currently open for registration, and when do they start?" (Addressed by the Tournaments Page).
  • "Where can I find the official rankings and total winnings for the top-rated players on the platform?" (Addressed by the Players Page Leaderboard).
  • "I'm experiencing an issue with my tournament registration; how do I contact support quickly?" (Addressed by the Connect Page contact form and FAQ).

4. Color Schema

The color scheme uses a dark background with vibrant neon accents to create a high-contrast, energetic, and professional esports aesthetic.

Name Hex Code Sample Usage
Primary Background #0F0F15 Main background color (body, header, footer).
Text/Base #FFFFFF Main text color (paragraphs, labels).
Accent Red (Live/Action) #FF3366 Primary CTA buttons, live indicators, and critical links.
Accent Blue (Neon) #00E0FF Borders, card headlines, and secondary accents.
Accent Orange (Highlight/Reward) #FF9900 Active navigation links, section title markers, and prize money data.

5. Typography

We use a single, highly versatile font family to maintain a clean, modern, and bold look across all page elements.

  • Font Family: Roboto (Sans-serif)
  • Usage: Used for all headings (h1, h2), body text, and navigation links.
  • Example Heading: THE ELITE. THE GRIND.
  • Example Body Text: The site plan serves as a blueprint for designing and developing your project.
